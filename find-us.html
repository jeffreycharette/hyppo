A Little More Info

Parking Downtown St. Augustine:

During off-peak times, the Toques lot on Hypolita St. is the closest option and provides city-metered parking (monitored only between 8am-5pm), but this is a small lot that fills quickly. Besides the scatterred street-parking that is sparse, the city-owned parking garage on Orange St. is likely the next best bet. We are about a 3-block walk from the garage heading south, either along St. George or Avenida Menendez to Charlotte.
