The Hyppo Popsicles

The Hyppo Cafe is St. Augustine's freshest cafe. Featuring gourmet sandwiches and salads, Bold Bean coffees and espresso, tea, fresh squeezed orange juice, agave sodas, baked goods, confections, and fresh homemade popsicles.

  • CURRENT PALETAS FLAVORS

    (UPDATED OFTEN)

    Banilla Vanana

    Fresh bananas puréed with Madagascar vanilla. This simple combo will help you forget all about those fake yellow banana flavored pops of yesteryear.

    Coconut Coconut

    Although coconut is one of the most classic paleta flavors, this combination of fresh coconut pieces and coconut milk brings a depth and richness of flavor uncommon in other coconut-branded treats, which explains why it’s such a local favorite.

    Key Lime

    Smooth, sweet, creamy, and tart tart tart – everything a good key lime pie should be. This fresh pop-translation of the classic Florida dessert delivers in all categories.

    Orange Cream

    This pop brings a sense of nostalgia to many Americans, being one of the most popular frozen treats on sticks in an era past. Now you can try it again, updated with fresh Valencia and Navel oranges.

    Mexican Hot Chocolate

    A tradition brought through the ages from the ancient Mayans which includes a blend of cocoa, cinnamon, and cayenne cooked down into a creamy infusion.

    Pineapple Cilantro

    One of our freshest-tasting and most popular flavors, this paleta blends fresh pineapple with a large helping of fresh cilantro to create an incredible frozen combination which, like the herb itself, you’ll likely either really love or really hate.

  • Cucumber Lemon Mint

    Fresh local cucumbers sweetened with cane sugar and tempered with lemon and fresh mint, this is one of the most refreshing pops this earth has to offer.

    Datil Strawberry

    Our most classic St. Augustine flavor, inspired by two centuries of Minorcan influence. We are happy to be able to make this variety again with fresh, sweet winter strawberries from Plant City and fresh datil peppers from, well, here of course. This pop is sweet, fruity, and spicy hot, showcasing the complex datil flavor on a lush strawberry palate.

    Riesling Pear

    Fresh green Bartlett pears blanched in a bath of sweet Riesling wine. Deep sweetness moves to a more lofty, complex finish, with that delightful pear texture shining through.

    Elvis Presley

    The King’s favorite: bananas, peanut butter, and honey – all balanced to create a scrumptious treat that will also fill out your daily food pyramid quite nicely.

    Mango Habanero

    This truly tropical combination brings smooth, cold sweet mango followed by a delightful chilli fire chaser. Once you start the cold/hot cycle, it's best not to stop.
