Customer Support FAQ

Our internet is down, and we cannot update our menu!

Please contact your internet provider on the status of your service. The menu will continue to be displayed with your last update. Once connected to the internet, try to make changes to your menu. If changes cannot be made, reset your device.

  • To reset a Samsung device power on and off with the remote.
  • To reset a HDMI stick, disconnect it from the power outlet on device or the wall and reconnect, then wait 3 minutes.

If this does not solve the problem, please contact customer support at 904.495.0616.

What happens if the power goes out?

We recommend using a surge protector with a battery-powered backup that turns on in the event of a power outage. That being said as soon as power and internet return your device will reboot automatically.

Our menu will not update even though our internet connection is online.

First, try resetting your device.

  • To reset a Samsung device power on and off with the remote.
  • To reset a HDMI stick, disconnect it from the power outlet on device or the wall and reconnect, you should see a loader within 15 seconds if the screen is powered on.

If this does not solve the problem, please contact customer support at 904.495.0616.

Our monitors are not displaying the menu.

Please check that:

  • The monitors are powered on.
  • No breakers are tripped in the electrical box.
  • Your backup battery system is powered on.

If you have a HDMI stick also check the following.

  • The HDMI stick is connected to the monitor.
  • The monitor is on the correct video input.

If this does not solve the problem, please contact customer support at 904.495.0616.

My duplicated items will not show up on my menu.

If duplicated items are not being displayed on your menu, click the gear icon in the top right corner of the editor window, then click save, and your menu should refresh.

If this does not work try resetting your device.

  • To reset a Samsung device power on and off with the remote.
  • To reset a HDMI stick, disconnect it from the power outlet on device or the wall and reconnect, you should see a loader within 15 seconds if the screen is powered on.

If this does not solve the problem, please contact customer support at 904.495.0616.

How do I reorder items?

To reorder items, go to your editor page and sign in. Then click on the red pencil next to the item that you would like to move. Scroll down to the ‘order’ field and type the number of the position where you would like that item to appear. Click save and your items will reorder.
